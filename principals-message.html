A Word From Our Principal

Dear Students, Parents
& Well-Wishers

Welcome to The Gurukul High — a place where every child is cherished, every dream is nurtured, and every success is celebrated.

It is my profound privilege and honour to serve as Principal of this wonderful institution. Since our founding in 1999, Gurukula Vidya Samsthe has stood as a beacon of quality education in K.R. Nagar, Mysuru — guided by our motto: Learn | Lead | Light.

We believe that the true purpose of education is not merely to prepare children for examinations, but to equip them with the values, skills, and wisdom they need to lead purposeful, meaningful lives. Our faculty, support staff, and management work tirelessly each day to make this a reality for every single student — from Nursery to Class X.

The Karnataka State Syllabus we follow ensures that our students are deeply grounded in local culture, language, and context, even as they develop the broader competencies to succeed in any walk of life.

To our students: be curious, be kind, and never stop learning. To our parents: thank you for trusting us with your most precious treasure. Together, we are building a brighter future — one child at a time.

"A school that educates the mind without educating the heart is no education at all. At The Gurukul High, we educate both."

— Smt. Kamala Devi R., Principal
