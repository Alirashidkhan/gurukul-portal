About The Gurukul High

A Legacy of Learning & Leadership in K.R. Nagar, Mysuru

Founded in 1999, The Gurukul High – Gurukula Vidya Samsthe has been a trusted institution in K.R. Nagar, Mysuru(D), Karnataka. Our school offers Nursery through Secondary (Class X) and blends modern pedagogy with India's timeless Gurukul tradition.

With dedicated teachers, excellent infrastructure, and a nurturing environment, The Gurukul High prepares students not just for academic success, but for a meaningful and purposeful life — grounded in the motto: Learn | Lead | Light.

Academic Programs

Nurturing Every Child from Nursery to Class X

The Gurukul High offers a complete schooling journey — Nursery, Elementary, and Secondary — building knowledge, character, and confidence at every stage.

Nursery & Pre-Primary

Nursery · LKG · UKG

A warm, play-based learning environment that sparks curiosity, builds early language skills, and helps the youngest learners thrive with joy and confidence.

  • Play-based & activity learning
  • Early literacy & numeracy
  • Rhymes, art & storytelling
  • Social skills & independence
Learn More

Elementary School

Classes I – V

A structured yet engaging curriculum that builds strong foundational skills in language, mathematics, science, and social studies through interactive learning methods.

  • English, Kannada & Hindi
  • Mathematics & EVS
  • Yoga, arts & craft
  • Value education & moral science
Learn More

Upper Elementary

Classes VI – VIII

A rigorous academic framework building conceptual understanding, analytical thinking, and communication skills — the bridge from elementary to secondary.

  • Science, Maths & Social Science
  • Language enrichment programs
  • Leadership & debate activities
  • Computer science basics

Secondary School

Classes IX – X (SSLC)

Comprehensive SSLC board preparation with expert coaching, mock exams, and personalised mentoring to help every student achieve their best in Class X.

  • Career orientation workshops

Our Facilities

World-Class Infrastructure for Inspired Learning

Our campus in K.R. Nagar, Mysuru is designed to provide an ideal learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities across academics, arts, and athletics.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs for hands-on experimental learning.

Computer Lab

High-speed internet-enabled computer labs with 150+ workstations and coding infrastructure.

Central Library

Over 25,000 books, e-journals, and a dedicated digital reading zone for students and staff.

Sports Complex

Multi-sport outdoor grounds, covered basketball courts, a swimming pool, and a gymnasium.

Arts & Music Studio

Dedicated studios for dance, music, painting, and drama with professional equipment.

Transport

GPS-enabled school bus fleet covering major areas of K.R. Nagar and surrounding areas of Mysuru.

Health & Wellness

On-campus medical room with a qualified nurse, yoga sessions, and counselling support.

Cafeteria & Dining

Hygienic, nutritious meals and snacks prepared fresh daily in our modern school cafeteria.
