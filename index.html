About Gurukul High School

A Legacy of Learning & Leadership in Mysore

Founded in 1965, Gurukul High School in Mysore has been a beacon of quality education in Karnataka for nearly six decades. Nestled in the culturally rich heritage city of Mysore, our school blends the best of modern pedagogy with India's timeless Gurukul tradition.

CBSE affiliated and fully equipped with world-class facilities, Gurukul High School prepares students not just for academic success, but for a meaningful and purposeful life — grounded in ethics, compassion, and intellectual curiosity.

Our Facilities

World-Class Infrastructure for Inspired Learning

Our expansive campus in Mysore is designed to provide an ideal learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities across academics, arts, and athletics.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs for hands-on experimental learning.

Computer Lab

High-speed internet-enabled computer labs with 150+ workstations and coding infrastructure.

Central Library

Over 25,000 books, e-journals, and a dedicated digital reading zone for students and staff.

Sports Complex

Multi-sport outdoor grounds, covered basketball courts, a swimming pool, and a gymnasium.

Arts & Music Studio

Dedicated studios for dance, music, painting, and drama with professional equipment.

Transport

GPS-enabled school bus fleet covering major areas of Mysore city and surrounding districts.

Health & Wellness

On-campus medical room with a qualified nurse, yoga sessions, and counselling support.

Cafeteria & Dining

Hygienic, nutritious meals and snacks prepared fresh daily in our modern school cafeteria.
