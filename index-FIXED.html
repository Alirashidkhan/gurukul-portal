FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to go live?

We guarantee go-live within 48 hours of signup. Our onboarding team will call you, collect your existing data (student lists, staff records, fee structures from Excel or your old system), configure EduCore to match your institution's setup and provide a live training session for your staff — all within two days.

Can we import data from our old software or Excel?

Yes — we support data migration from Excel, CSV, Fedena, Entab, and most other school ERP systems. Our team handles the migration for you at no extra cost. You don't need to re-enter any data manually.

Is EduCore suitable for coaching centres and colleges, not just schools?

Absolutely. EduCore is used by K-12 schools, junior colleges, degree colleges, coaching centres and corporate training institutes. The platform is fully configurable — you can enable only the modules relevant to your institution type.

How is pricing calculated? Are there hidden charges?

Pricing is based on the number of enrolled students per academic year. All modules are included — there are no per-module add-on fees. Setup, data migration, training and ongoing support are also included. The only additional cost would be SMS credits if you use our bulk SMS feature.

Is our data secure? Where is it stored?

Your data is stored on enterprise-grade cloud servers in India (compliant with Indian data localisation requirements). We use SSL encryption, daily automated backups, and role-based access control so only authorised staff can access sensitive data. Our audit dashboard tracks every login and data change for full transparency.

Do parents and students need to install an app?

No app installation required. The parent and student portals are fully web-based and mobile-responsive — they work perfectly on any smartphone browser. We also offer optional Android and iOS apps for institutions on the Professional and Enterprise plans.

What support do we get after going live?

Every institution gets a dedicated WhatsApp support group with our team, a help desk portal, and access to our video training library. Professional and Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager. We're an Indian company — our support team speaks Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu, Malayalam and English.

Do we own our data? What happens if we leave?

Yes — 100%. Your student records, fee history, staff data, attendance logs, marks and all reports belong entirely to your institution. You can export everything in standard formats (Excel, CSV, PDF) at any time from within the platform — no support ticket needed, no waiting, no fee. If you ever decide to leave EduCore (though we're confident you won't), you take every byte of your data with you. No lock-in. Ever.
